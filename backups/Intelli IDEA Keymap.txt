--------------------------
My Intelli IDEA KeyMap
--------------------------

ctrl+j     |  Left
ctrl+k     |  Right
ctrl+i     |  Up
ctrl+m     |  Down
ctrl+h     |  Move caret To line head
ctrl+;     |  Move caret To line end
ctrl+u(+shift) | move caret to previous word (with selection)
ctrl+o(+shift) | move caret to next word (with selection)


ctrl+c     |  copy
ctrl+s     |  save
ctrl+x     |  cut
ctrl+v     |  paste
ctrl+r     |  replace
ctrl+z     |  undo
ctrl+shift+z     |  redo
alt+↓/↑    |  整行向下/上移动
alt+shift+↓|  整行向下复制
alt+s      |  reformat file

ctrl+点击  |  查看定义
alt+d      |  查看定义
ctrl+e     |  快速跳到下一个错误或警告
ctrl+f     |  find
ctrl+q     | 全局查找
alt+h      |  type hierarchy
alt+←/→    |  返回上/下一个浏览位置
alt+enter   | 快捷操作

ctrl+d     |  send EOF
alt+t      |  划词翻译
alt+enter  |  快速修复/show intention action
shift,shift|  全局查找文件
